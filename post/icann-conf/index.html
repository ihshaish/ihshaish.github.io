31st International Conference on Artificial Neural Networks at UWE.

We hosted and organised the 31st International Conference on Artificial Neural Networks at UWE. My undergraduate student, Reece Nicholles, presnted his paper on “Problem Classification for Tailored Helpdesk Auto-replies” and Rafet Durgut, visiting researcher, on “Analysing the Predictivity of Features to Characterise the Search Space”.
