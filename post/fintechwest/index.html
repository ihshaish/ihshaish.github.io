FinTech West Talent Showcase Event

On Wednesday 12th October, FinTech West hosted our inaugural FinTech Talent Showcase event, hosted in partnership with the University of Bristol and the University of the West of England. Martin Palmer, Marketing Director at Clarke Willmott, opened the event and welcomed the audience, reflecting on how Clarke Willmott had been involved in the creation of FinTech West, which was originally known as Bristol FinTech.
